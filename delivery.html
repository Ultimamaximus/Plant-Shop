Delivery Policy

CONTACTLESS DELIVERY


To help ensure the health and safety of our customers, our team, and the broader community we have introduced Contactless Delivery as our default delivery option. Contactless Delivery means:

  • We’ll deliver your item(s) to the front door of your house or apartment

  • We’ll knock/buzz, call or text, and will greet you at your door while maintaining a six-foot social distance

Some multi-tenant buildings are restricting access to non-residents. We are supportive of these measures, and in these cases our delivery team will deliver to the closest point to your home that they are safely able to.


DELIVERY SCHEDULES


Delivery days are Wednesday and Friday. Orders received on Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday will be scheduled for delivery on Wednesday. Orders received on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday will be scheduled for delivery on Friday. To change delivery day, please go to the Contact Us page.
